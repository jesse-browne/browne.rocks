How he popped the question...

As the night unfolded there were subtle hints that Friday September 26 was going to be special. Jesse and I had been planning to leave town for the weekend and visit some beautiful national park. I had ordered a dress online earlier in the week and was nervous to leave town and not have it delivered first.

As we waited for the package to arrive and it got later, Jesse brought out a nice bottle of red wine and we snacked. We decided that when my dress arrived I was just going to put it on and we were going to go out to a nice dinner. It was fairly late by the time we were ready to go, probably half past 9 and we caught a cab to Fillmore, a charming street in the middle of the city.

We enjoyed a lovely dinner in the ambiance of Dosa, a chic southern style Indian food restaurant. Afterwards while sipping on a champagne cognac and avidly talking about adventures past and future, he pulled out a ring box. It was wonderfully low key and happened so fast, but I remember his words were – “Shannon, will you be my queen? Will you marry me?” To which I said, “Yes!”
